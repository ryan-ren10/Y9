1. Eternal Atake(Deluxe)

Best Song: Bigger Than Life

An album that rapper artist Lil Uzi Vert had been working on for more than a couple of years, this album released in 2020 is the artist's latest major album drop since his infamous album in 2017 titled Luv is Rage 2. It contains songs that are ALL BANGERS and you can vibe to them on the daily. The songs in these albums are all "head-bopping" tunes that bring good energy.

2. ASTROWORLD

Best Song: SICKOMODE

These songs by Travis Scott(while over-played) are all still classics. This album most certaintly lived up to the hype!

3. Goodbye and Good Riddance

Best Song: Lucid Dreams

Another over-played album, but objectively is still an incredible album that will get you in your feelings if that's what your looking for. This album best represented Juice Wrld as an artist and as a being(Rip Juice).
